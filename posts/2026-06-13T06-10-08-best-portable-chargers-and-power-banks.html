Buying Guide: What to Look for in a Portable Charger or Power Bank

When choosing a portable charger or power bank, there are several factors to consider. Here are some key features to look for:

  • Capacity: Look for a power bank with a high capacity, measured in milliampere-hours (mAh). A higher capacity means more charges for your devices.
  • Size and weight: Consider a power bank that is compact and lightweight, making it easy to carry on the go.
  • USB ports: Look for a power bank with multiple USB ports, including USB-A, USB-C, and Micro-USB.
  • Charging speed: Consider a power bank with high-speed charging capability, measured in watts (W).
  • Wireless charging: If you want to charge your devices wirelessly, look for a power bank with wireless charging capability.
