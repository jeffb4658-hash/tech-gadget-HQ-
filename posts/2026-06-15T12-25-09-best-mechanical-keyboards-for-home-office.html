What to Look for in a Mechanical Keyboard

When shopping for a mechanical keyboard, there are several factors to consider. These include the type of switches used, the keyboard's durability and build quality, and any additional features such as backlighting or macro keys. Here are some key things to look for:

  • Switch type: Mechanical keyboards use individual switches under each key, which can be either linear, tactile, or clicky.
  • Durability: Look for keyboards with a sturdy build and high-quality switches that can withstand heavy use.
  • Comfort: Consider a keyboard with a ergonomic design and a comfortable keycap size.
  • Features: Some keyboards come with additional features such as backlighting, macro keys, and media controls.
