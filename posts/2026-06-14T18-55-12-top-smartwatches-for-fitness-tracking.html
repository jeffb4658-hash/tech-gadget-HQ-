What to Look for in a Fitness Tracking Smartwatch

When choosing a smartwatch for fitness tracking, there are several factors to consider. These include heart rate monitoring, GPS tracking, water resistance, and compatibility with your smartphone. Additionally, consider the battery life, display, and user interface to ensure that it meets your needs.
