What to Look for in a Fitness Smartwatch

When choosing a smartwatch for fitness tracking, there are several factors to consider. These include the type of activities you want to track, the level of accuracy you need, and the additional features that are important to you, such as heart rate monitoring, GPS, and water resistance.

Key Features to Consider

  • Activity tracking (steps, distance, calories burned)
  • Heart rate monitoring
  • GPS tracking
  • Water resistance
  • Notification support (calls, texts, emails)
  • Music control and storage
  • Battery life
