Buying Guide: What to Look for in a Mechanical Keyboard

When choosing a mechanical keyboard for your home office, there are several factors to consider. Here are some key things to look for:

  • Switch type: Mechanical keyboards use physical switches under each key, which can vary in terms of actuation force, sound, and feel. Popular switch types include Cherry MX, Kailh, and Outemu.
  • Keyboard layout: Mechanical keyboards come in a range of layouts, including full-size, tenkeyless, and compact 60% designs. Consider the amount of space you have available and your personal typing preferences.
  • Durability: Look for keyboards with high-quality construction, durable switches, and a sturdy frame.
  • Features: Consider the features that are important to you, such as customizable lighting, macro keys, and wireless connectivity.
