Buying Guide: What to Consider When Choosing a Mechanical Keyboard

When selecting a mechanical keyboard, consider the following factors:

  • Switch type: Choose from popular switch types such as Cherry MX, Kailh, or Romer-G, considering factors such as actuation force, travel distance, and sound level.
  • Ergonomics: Consider a keyboard with a built-in wrist rest, adjustable height, and ergonomic design to reduce fatigue and discomfort.
  • Customization options: Look for keyboards with customizable lighting, macro keys, and programmable keys to enhance your productivity and gaming experience.
  • Price: Mechanical keyboards can range from budget-friendly to premium, so set a budget and choose a keyboard that meets your needs and price range.
