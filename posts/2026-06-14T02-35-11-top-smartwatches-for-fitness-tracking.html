Buying Guide

When choosing a smartwatch for fitness tracking, consider the following factors:

  • Accuracy and reliability of GPS and heart rate monitoring
  • Compatibility with your smartphone and other devices
  • Battery life and charging requirements
  • Water resistance and durability
  • Additional features, such as music storage and mobile payments
