Buying Guide

When choosing a mechanical keyboard for your home office, there are several factors to consider. Here are some tips to help you make the right decision:

  • Switch type: Choose a keyboard with switches that suit your typing style. Linear switches are great for gamers, while tactile switches are better for typists.
  • Keycap quality: Look for keyboards with high-quality keycaps that are durable and resistant to wear and tear.
  • Ergonomics: Consider a keyboard with a built-in wrist rest or a compact tenkeyless design to reduce strain on your wrists and hands.
  • Customization options: Choose a keyboard with customizable RGB lighting, macro keys, and dedicated media controls to enhance your typing experience.
