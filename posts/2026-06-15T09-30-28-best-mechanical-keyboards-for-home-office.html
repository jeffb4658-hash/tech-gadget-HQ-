Why Mechanical Keyboards are a Must-Have for Home Office

Mechanical keyboards offer several benefits over traditional membrane keyboards, including better tactile feedback, improved typing accuracy, and increased durability. They're also customizable, allowing you to personalize your typing experience with different switch types and keycap designs.

Buying Guide: What to Look for in a Mechanical Keyboard

When buying a mechanical keyboard, there are several factors to consider. Here are some key features to look for:

  • Switch type: Cherry MX, Kailh, or Romer-G switches are popular options.
  • Keycap design: Look for keyboards with customizable keycaps or ergonomic designs.
  • Customizable lighting: RGB lighting can enhance the typing experience.
  • Wrist rest: A built-in wrist rest can improve typing comfort.
  • Durability: Look for keyboards with durable construction and long-lasting switches.
