Buying Guide

When choosing a smartwatch for fitness tracking, there are several factors to consider. Here are some key features to look for:

  • GPS tracking: Look for a smartwatch with built-in GPS tracking, which allows you to track your runs, hikes, and bike rides with accuracy.
  • Heart rate monitoring: A smartwatch with heart rate monitoring can help you track your fitness progress and alert you to any potential health issues.
  • Water resistance: If you plan to use your smartwatch for swimming or water sports, look for a model that's water-resistant up to 50 meters or more.
  • Integration with fitness apps: Consider a smartwatch that integrates with popular fitness apps, such as Strava, MyFitnessPal, or Apple Health.
  • Battery life: Look for a smartwatch with a long-lasting battery life, especially if you plan to use it for extended periods of time.
