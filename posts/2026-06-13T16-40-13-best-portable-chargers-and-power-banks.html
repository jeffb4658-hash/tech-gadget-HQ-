What to Look for in a Portable Charger or Power Bank

When shopping for a portable charger or power bank, there are several factors to consider. Here are some of the key things to look for:

  • Capacity: Measured in milliampere-hours (mAh), this refers to how much power the charger or power bank can hold. A higher capacity means you can charge your devices more times before needing to recharge the charger or power bank itself.
  • Size and Weight: If you plan to carry the charger or power bank with you, look for one that is compact and lightweight.
  • Number of Ports: If you have multiple devices to charge at once, look for a charger or power bank with multiple USB ports.
  • Speed: Look for a charger or power bank that supports fast charging, which can charge your devices up to 75% faster than standard charging.
