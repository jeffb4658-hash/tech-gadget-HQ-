Buying Guide

When choosing a smartwatch for fitness tracking, there are several factors to consider:

  • Compatibility: Make sure the smartwatch is compatible with your device, whether it's an iPhone or Android smartphone.
  • GPS: If you're a runner or cyclist, look for a smartwatch with built-in GPS for accurate distance tracking.
  • Heart Rate Monitoring: If you're interested in tracking your heart rate, look for a smartwatch with built-in heart rate monitoring.
  • Water Resistance: If you plan to use your smartwatch for swimming or water sports, look for one with water resistance up to 50 meters or more.
  • Battery Life: Consider a smartwatch with a long battery life, especially if you plan to use it for extended periods.
