Buying Guide

When choosing a mechanical keyboard for your home office, consider the following factors:

  • Switch type: Cherry MX, Romer-G, and Kailh are popular switch types, each offering unique tactile and auditory feedback.
  • Keyboard size: Tenkeyless, full-size, and compact keyboards are available, depending on your desk space and typing preferences.
  • Customization options: Look for keyboards with customizable lighting, macro capabilities, and media controls.
  • Connectivity: Wireless, USB, and Bluetooth connectivity options are available, depending on your needs and preferences.

By considering these factors and choosing the right mechanical keyboard for your needs, you'll improve your typing experience and boost your productivity.
