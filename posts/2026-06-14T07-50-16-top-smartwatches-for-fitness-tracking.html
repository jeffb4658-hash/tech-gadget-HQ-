What to Look for in a Fitness Tracking Smartwatch

When shopping for a smartwatch for fitness tracking, there are several key features to consider. These include GPS tracking, heart rate monitoring, water resistance, and compatibility with your smartphone. You'll also want to consider the battery life, display quality, and overall design of the watch.
