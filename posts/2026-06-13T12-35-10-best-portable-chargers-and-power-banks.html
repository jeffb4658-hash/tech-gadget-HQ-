What to Look for in a Portable Charger or Power Bank

Before we dive into our recommendations, let's discuss some key factors to consider when choosing a portable charger or power bank. These include:

  • Capacity: Measured in milliampere-hours (mAh), this determines how much charge the power bank can hold.
  • Output: Look for a power bank with multiple output ports, including USB-A, USB-C, and QuickCharge.
  • Size and Weight: A compact and lightweight design is essential for portability.
  • Material and Build Quality: A durable and rugged design can withstand rough handling.
