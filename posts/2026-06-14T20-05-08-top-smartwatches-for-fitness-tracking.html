Buying Guide

When choosing a smartwatch for fitness tracking, there are several factors to consider. Here are some key things to keep in mind:

  • GPS and location tracking: If you plan to use your smartwatch for outdoor activities like running or hiking, look for a device with built-in GPS and location tracking.
  • Heart rate monitoring: If you want to track your heart rate during exercise, look for a device with built-in heart rate monitoring.
  • Water resistance: If you plan to use your smartwatch for swimming or water sports, look for a device with water resistance or waterproofing.
  • Compatibility: Make sure the smartwatch is compatible with your smartphone or other devices.
