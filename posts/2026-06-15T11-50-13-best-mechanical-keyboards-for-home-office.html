Buying Guide

When choosing a mechanical keyboard for your home office, there are several factors to consider. Here are some key considerations to keep in mind:

  • Switch type: Mechanical keyboards come with a range of switch types, including linear, tactile, and clicky. Consider the type of switch that suits your typing style and preferences.
  • Keyboard size: Mechanical keyboards come in a range of sizes, from compact tenkeyless designs to full-size keyboards. Consider the size of your desk and the amount of space you have available.
  • Customization options: Consider the level of customization you need, including backlighting, macro keys, and media controls.
  • Build quality: Look for keyboards with excellent build quality, including durable materials and a sturdy construction.
  • Price: Mechanical keyboards can range in price from under $100 to over $200. Consider your budget and the features you need.
